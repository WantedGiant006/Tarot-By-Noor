We're here to answer all your questions

Whether you're new to tarot or looking to enhance your readings, this section will guide you through everything you need to know about our deck and how to use it.

Is Tarot Fortune-Telling ?

Tarot is not about predicting a fixed future. Instead, it reveals potential paths and empowers you to shape your own destiny.

Do You Need to Believe in It for It to Work ?

Tarot doesn't require belief to work. The cards are tools, and their insights often resonate regardless of skepticism.

It's Scary or Negative!

Tarot is not about doom and gloom. Even challenging cards, like The Tower or Death, represent transformation, growth, and the end of cycles, making way for new beginnings.
